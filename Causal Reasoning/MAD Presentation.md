**Formula for causal relations: (Rehder)**
$$P(E=1│c_1…c_n )=1-(1-b){∏_{c_i}^n}(1-m)^{c_i}$$


Why deviations occurr?:
- Similarity based judgements or heuristics (Rehder, 2014) - Mental Shorcuts
- Causal links and hidden nodes (Park & Sloma, 2013) - Other subjective and biased factors
- Limited cognitive resources (Davis & Rehder, 2020) - Sampling
- Generalized uncertainty (Marchant, 2023)

**Uncertainty Formula:** [[Bayes Theorem]]
$$P(B│A)=P(B│A,H)P(H│A)+P(B│A,H′)P(H′│A)$$
Where P(H') = 1 - P(H)
